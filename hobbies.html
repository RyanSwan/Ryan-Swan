Hobbies

This section is a celebration of my athletic pursuits and the competitive spirit that drives me both on and off the field. Join me as I share the highlights and the trophies of my sporting life.

Football

Years of competing in soccer at a high level underage for respected clubs like St Kevin's Boys and Belvedere FC have shaped a strong foundation in teamwork and sportsmanship. These experiences include playing against Premier League academies, such as Newcastle and Leeds, which are both challenging and rewarding. I love watching Sky Sports analysis of football, as the pundits provide great entertainment!

Golf

From the age of 8, the greens and fairways of Clontarf Golf Club have been a second home, with my proudest achievement being named Junior Golfer of the Year in 2019. With a playing handicap of 14, the golf journey reflects a blend of skill, passion, and dedication to the sport. It is a great experience playing a round with family and friends! I found Rick Shiels' tips to be quite helpful and entertaining to watch, give them a watch!

  • Clontarf GC
  • Rick Shiels

GAA

Whitehall Colmcille GAA has been a constant presence, providing a sense of community and a competitive edge. The sport has been a platform for showcasing teamwork and achieving numerous accolades over the years. Whitehall represents my local parish, so there is a great sense of pride playing for them. Here, I have made friends for life!
